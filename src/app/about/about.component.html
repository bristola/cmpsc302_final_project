About

We are a small group who have developed this company during college.

This company started in a dorm room at Allegheny college. James Burnette, Joe Nagel, and Austin Bristol enjoyed the new technology of cryptocurrency, and developed the main idea for the company. In the beginning of 2018, we started developing our software to automatically trade coins for profit. Our focus was to lower the risk of trading in this volatile market by holding our capital in US dollars as much time as possible. By implementing a arbitrage algorithm, we have beem able to generate profits in the cryptocurrency markets while lowering investors' risks.

In spring of 2018, Azariel Technologies entered the Big Idea Competition at Allegheny College. In the end, we won the competition after pitching our company to an experienced board of judges. Since then, we have created an LLC as well as continue to develop more software to aid our trading. By expanding into more market analyses, as well as creating more refined code and safeguards, we have been able to enhance system.
